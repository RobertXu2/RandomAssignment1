My Past

Making YouTube Videos

During my free time, I created and uploaded Roblox videos to YouTube. This was back in Year 10, I interacted with technology softwares such as Adobe Premiere Pro, Photoshop etc to edit videos. The channel is now inactive but the highest video reached close to 100k views. It also taught me how to engage with viewers and manage a small YouTube channel.

Writing My First Line Of Code

My first experience with coding was in Roblox using Roblox Lua. I created a small game which, although now inactive, reached over 50k visits. ; a huge milestone for me at the time. I went on to create other games that were not as successful, but each one taught me something new. Through this, I learned how to write and structure Lua scripts for game mechanics, as well as fundamental programming concepts such as loops, conditionals, and events.
